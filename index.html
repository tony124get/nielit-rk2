WELCOME TO NIELIT IMPHAL OFFICIAL WEBSITE

The National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT), Imphal Centre, operates under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India. Located at Akampat, Imphal East, the centre plays a key role in promoting IT literacy, professional training, and skill development in the region.

Since its inception, NIELIT Imphal has provided high-quality education in various IECT fields, including Computer Science, Electronics, Cyber Security, and Digital Forensics. The institute has earned a reputation for its industry-relevant curriculum, well-equipped infrastructure, and commitment to academic excellence.

NIELIT Imphal is dedicated to supporting the National Skill Development Mission and Digital India vision. The centre regularly organizes workshops, training programs, and certification courses to empower youth, professionals, and government employees.

With experienced faculty, modern laboratories, and a learner-centric approach, NIELIT Imphal continues to be a beacon of digital education in the North East. It aims to create an inclusive learning environment where innovation, ethics, and technology intersect to build a smarter India.

ACCREDITATION

NIELIT Imphal is an accredited institution under MeitY, Govt. of India, with the authority to conduct formal and non-formal training programs in IECT domains. The centre has received national-level recognition for its contributions toward digital empowerment.

It offers recognized certifications including 'O' Level, 'A' Level, and specialized programs in Software, Hardware, Networking, and emerging tech domains. NIELIT qualifications are accepted by various government and private sector employers across the country.
